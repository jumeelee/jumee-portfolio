About Me

Hi, I’m Jumee Lee, a designer based in Canada with a focus on typography and clean, thoughtful design.

I explore how type, layout, and visual hierarchy can work together to communicate ideas clearly and create engaging experiences.

My work balances aesthetics with functionality, aiming to create visuals that are both meaningful and easy to understand.
